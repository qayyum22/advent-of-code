Our Team

With Twelfth Dream, you're not just getting one developer working for you, you have a team of specialists whose job it is to deliver software products on our 6-step project process.

Along with our project managers who are committed to delivering projects on time and within budget, our team includes:

Business Analyst

Gathers requirements to design solutions, test products and deliver to the customer.

UX Developer

Designs the steps users take to achieve a goal or accomplish a task, builds prototypes and mockups.

UI Developer

Designs and builds the page that users see via browser or mobile app, ensuring the design is responsive.

Back-end Developer

Implements the logic behind the products you're using, processes and handles your requests.

Database Specialist

Designs and builds databases to store data and respond to queries.

Development IT Operations

Builds virtual servers, sets up scheduling, backup, and monitors them.
